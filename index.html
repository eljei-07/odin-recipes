What is a Full Course Meal?

A full course meal consist of different meal courses arrange from light plates, continuing with richer dishes, and finishing with delicate ones.

Five Full Course Meal

A five course meal consists of appetizer, soup, salad, main dish, and a dessert.

  1. Appetizer - this course features small cuts of meat, seasonal vegetables, starches, and sauces.

    Sample recipe for appetizer is Chicken Phyllo Cups

  2. Soup - primarily liquid food, generally served warm or hot that is made by combining ingredients of meat or vegetables with stock, milk, or water.

  4. Main Course/Dish - Typically, the main course is the meal that is the heaviest, heartiest, and most intricate or substantial on the menu.

    Sample recipe for a main course is Paprika Beef Steaks with Chimichurri Sauce and Wedges

  5. Dessert - he course consists of sweet foods, such as confections, and possibly a beverage such as dessert wine and liqueur.

    Sample recipe is Strawberry Parfait
